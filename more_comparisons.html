DenseDPO: Fine-Grained Temporal

Preference Optimization for Video Diffusion Models

Qualitative Results

We show text prompts and generated videos from the pre-trained, Supervised Fine-Tuned (SFT), VanillaDPO, StructuralDPO, and our DenseDPO aligned models.
[Back to main page]

  • Pre-trained model often generates deformed limbs and objects.
  • SFT leads to almost no change in the results.
  • VanillaDPO reduces distortions, but with significantly lower dynamics.
  • StructuralDPO improves the dynamics, but degrades the visual quality and reintroduces some artifacts.
  • Our DenseDPO model generates high-quality videos with correct object dynamics and realistic details.
